jurisdiction and it consists of Company Income Tax, Education tax, NITDEF tax and Nigeria

assessable profit, NITDEF tax is a 1% levy on Profit before tax of the Bank, and Nigeria
Police Trust Fund Levy is 0.005% of Net profit.
